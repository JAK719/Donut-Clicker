Meet Fred
Fred the Baker has worked tirelessly for decades making donuts for the masses. Now he is ready to retire and needs you to take over the business. You must make donuts by clicking th big donut until you have enough to donuts to purchase another donut fryer to multiply your output, then hire extra bakers to make even more donuts.
